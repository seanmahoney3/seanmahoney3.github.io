About Me

An experienced and passionate sound recordist and designer - I have years of experience of on-set, location and foley recording and post-production designing and mixing. A love of music and sound-art has taken me on a creative journey from touring the world in bands and album engineering into film, commercial and television sound recording, mixing and designing.

A true musical ear combined with an acute attention to detail allows me to tackle any brief or project with professionalism, enthusiasm and, above all, a total devotion to the wonders of sound.

I enjoy projects of all sizes so don’t hesitate to get in touch.
